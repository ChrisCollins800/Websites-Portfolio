Activities and Facilities Nearby

Dingle is a vibrant coastal town with plenty to see and do. Some popular activities include hiking along the Dingle Way, surfing at Inch Beach, visiting the Dingle Oceanworld Aquarium, and exploring the Slea Head Drive. The town is also home to many pubs, restaurants, and shops, as well as a weekly farmers' market.

Rooms and Facilities

Our B&B in Dingle offers comfortable and spacious rooms with en-suite bathrooms, free Wi-Fi, and flat-screen TVs. Guests can enjoy a full Irish breakfast each morning in our dining room, and we also have a cozy lounge area with a fireplace for guests to relax in.

Check-In Times

Our standard check-in time is from 16:00 pm to 22:00 pm. If you require an earlier or later check-in time, please let us know in advance and we will do our best to accommodate your request.

B&B Ireland Inspection

Yes, our B&B is inspected and approved by B&B Ireland, ensuring that we meet the highest standards of quality and comfort.

Guide Dogs and Pets

Unfortunately, we do not allow pets at our B&B, except for guide dogs or service animals.

Smoking Restrictions

Our B&B is a smoke-free establishment. Smoking is not permitted anywhere on the premises, including in the guest rooms and common areas.

Weather Information

Dingle enjoys a mild, temperate climate with relatively consistent temperatures throughout the year. Summer temperatures average around 18°C (64°F), while winter temperatures typically range from 5-10°C (41-50°F). It can be quite rainy, so it's a good idea to bring appropriate clothing and footwear.
